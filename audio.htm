Music has always been a big part of my life and always will. I studied Game Development - Music and Sound at University of Skövde. The education combined composition, sound design, audio programming, recording, mixing and game analytics. Additionally I took two courses in Sound Engineering at University of Örebro.

My speciality is in combining sound and music with visuals and game mechanics. Very simple music or gameplay can be incredible enhanced when combined together. A musical piece that developes as a player in a game progresses or sound effects that doesn't just enhance the experience but is part of the gameplay. Developing an interactive soundscape requires skills in Sound Design, Programming and Game design.

I have a personal sound bank including sounds from ambient noises of cities and nature to abstract sound effects created with software. I also have a Sound Cloud account with my composed music.
